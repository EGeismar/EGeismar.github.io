Ethan Geismar

Biography

Ethan Geismar was born in New York, NY on November 11, 1997. He attended the Anderson School for grades K-8 and High School of American Studies at Lehman College for High School. He is currently a senior at Tufts University studying Computer Science Engineering.

In addition to his studies, Ethan has enjoyed many activities outside of the classroom, from recreational involvements such as WMFO (Tufts Radio) and Tufts Dance Collective, to more serious commitments such as the Tufts Financial Group and the Engineering Curriculum Student-Faculty Committee.

Ethan's professional pursuits lie at the intersection of finance and technology. His prior internships include roles in both fundamental and technology-oriented finance applications at Oppenheimer, Jefferies, and Redline Trading Solutions. Currently, he is working with peers on a capstone project for a major asset manager in Boston. After graduating, Ethan will be returning to Jefferies to work in their Equity Research group. More about Ethan's education and professional endeavors can be found on his resume.

In his free time, Ethan enjoys cooking, listening to podcasts, playing recreational sports, and spending time with friends and family. He is an avid music listener and explorer, and has a very cute mini labradoodle, Mischief.
